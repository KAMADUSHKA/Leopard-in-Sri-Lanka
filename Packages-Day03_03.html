Two or Three Day Tour – Safari, Ella, Nuwara Eliya & Kandy

You can also customize your own trip instead of choosing from the packages listed. Contact us to plan your unforgettable journey! You can also choose the order of the places you'd like to visit.

This scenic tour takes you from the coastal town of Galle into the lush hill country and cultural heart of Sri Lanka. Visit waterfalls, historic temples, tea plantations, and enjoy a wildlife safari. Perfect for travelers seeking nature, culture, and adventure.

📍 Tour Overview

  • Start Location: Galle
  • End Location: Galle
  • Duration: 3 Days, 2 Night
  • Accommodation: Can be arranged upon request

🗓️ Day 1 – Galle to Ella

  • Rakshithakanda Temple
  • Rawana Waterfall
  • Alla Waterfall
  • Nine Arch Bridge
  • Little Adam’s Peak
  • Dowa Temple
  • Overnight in Ella or nearby area

🗓️ Day 2 – Safari & Hill Country

  • Morning Safari at Yala or Udawalawe National Park
  • Mahamewnawa Meditation Monastery
  • Nuwara Eliya City Tour
  • Gregory Lake
  • Ramboda Waterfalls
  • Labookellie Tea Plantation & Factory
  • Overnight in Nuwara Eliya or Kandy

🗓️ Day 3 – Kandy to Colombo or Back to Galle

  • Optional Train Ride (Ella to Nanu Oya)
  • Kandy City Tour
  • Temple of the Tooth Relic
  • Kandy Cultural Dance Show
  • Gadaladeniya Rock Temple
  • Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage
  • End tour in Colombo or return to Galle

💰 Price

Contact us for pricing details. Prices vary based on group size, route, vehicle type, and accommodations.

🚗 Transportation

Travel in a private air-conditioned vehicle with an experienced driver. Enjoy a safe and smooth journey through lush valleys, hilltops, and countryside roads.

🍽️ Meals

Enjoy delicious local meals on both days. Let us know your preferences – vegetarian and special dietary options are available.

👥 Group Size

Ideal for couples, families, or small groups. Private tour options are available for a more personalized experience.
