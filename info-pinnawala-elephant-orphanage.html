Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage – The Sanctuary of Gentle Giants

Nestled along the banks of the Maha Oya River in central Sri Lanka, Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage is one of the island’s most beloved attractions. Established in 1975, this unique sanctuary is home to orphaned, injured, and abandoned elephants, offering visitors a rare chance to observe and interact with these magnificent animals in a protected environment.

Best Time to Visit

"The orphanage is open year-round, but the best time to visit is during feeding and bathing hours—typically between 9:00 AM and 1:00 PM. Watching the elephants bathe and play in the river is a heartwarming and unforgettable sight."

A Heartwarming Experience

Pinnawala is home to the largest herd of captive elephants in the world. Visitors can witness baby elephants being bottle-fed, see them roam in herds, and even watch them splash around during their daily river baths. The orphanage plays a vital role in conservation, breeding, and public education about elephant welfare.

Unlike in the wild, Pinnawala offers visitors the unique opportunity to get up close to elephants in a safe and ethical setting. From feeding sessions to river walks, every moment at the orphanage is filled with awe and admiration for these gentle giants. Guided tours provide educational insights into their behavior, history, and conservation efforts.

Why Visit Pinnawala?

🟢 Elephant Care & Conservation – Witness how orphaned and injured elephants are nurtured back to health.

🟢 River Bathing Rituals – TExperience the joy of watching elephants bathe and play in the river.

🟢 Family-Friendly Experience – A perfect destination for families and animal lovers of all ages.

🟢 Memorable Photo Opportunities – Get incredible shots of elephants up close in natural surroundings.


A visit to Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage is more than just a tour—it's a chance to connect with Sri Lanka’s wildlife in a meaningful and compassionate way. Whether you’re an animal lover, a conservation supporter, or simply a curious traveler, Pinnawala promises an enriching and unforgettable experience.
