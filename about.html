Top Leopard Safari Destinations in Sri Lanka

Explore the wild and witness the elusive Sri Lankan leopard in its natural habitat across the island’s best national parks.

01

Yala National Park

Yala is Sri Lanka’s most famous wildlife park and home to the highest density of leopards in the world. Spot these elusive big cats in their natural habitat.

02

Wilpattu National Park

Located in the northwest, Wilpattu is known for its unique "villus" (natural lakes) and thriving leopard population. A perfect destination for quiet, scenic safaris.

03

Horton Plains National Park

A misty highland plateau where leopards live among cloud forests and grasslands. Though rare to spot, Horton Plains is a hidden gem for wildlife enthusiasts.

04

Kumana National Park

Bordering Yala to the east, Kumana is less crowded but rich in wildlife. It offers a serene leopard-watching experience among wetlands and forests.
