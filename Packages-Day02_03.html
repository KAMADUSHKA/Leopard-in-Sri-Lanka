Two Day Safari Tour - Yala or Udawalawe Adventure

You can also customize your own trip instead of choosing from the packages listed. Contact us to plan your unforgettable journey! You can also choose the order of the places you'd like to visit.

Discover the best of Sri Lanka's culture, nature, and wildlife with our two-day guided tour. You can start either from Colombo or Galle, making it flexible for your travel plans. This immersive experience ends with a thrilling safari in either Yala or Udawalawe National Park.

📍 Option 1 – Start from Colombo

🗓️ Day 1 - Coastal Wonders
  • Madu River Safari
  • Turtle Conservation Farm Visit
  • Galle Dutch Fort Exploration
  • Relax at Mirissa Beach
  • Watch Traditional Stick Fishermen in Koggala
  • Overnight stay near Yala/Udawalawe
🗓️ Day 2 - Wildlife Safari
  • Early Morning Safari in Yala or Udawalawe National Park
  • Enjoy leopard, elephant, and wildlife sightings
  • Breakfast after the safari
  • Return journey to Colombo

📍 Option 2 – Start from Galle

🗓️ Day 1 - Ella Region Exploration
  • Visit Rakkithakanda Ancient Temple
  • Rawana Waterfall Stop
  • Hike to Little Adam's Peak
  • Nine Arch Bridge Visit
  • Ella City Tour & Scenic Drive
  • Explore Dowa Temple & Mahamewnawa Monastery
  • Overnight stay near Yala/Udawalawe
🗓️ Day 2 - Wildlife Safari
  • Morning Jeep Safari in Yala or Udawalawe
  • Wildlife spotting: leopards, elephants, birds, and more
  • Return journey to Galle in the afternoon

🦁 About the Safari

Choose your safari park:

  • Yala National Park – Famous for leopards and rich biodiversity
  • Udawalawe National Park – Known for elephants and open landscapes
